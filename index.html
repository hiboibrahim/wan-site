WHO ARE WE?

Welcome to WAN Cymru, where our mission is to empower women and families in our communities by giving them a strong voice in the issues that matter most. As a dedicated group of mothers and advocates, we are committed to placing the interests of our community at the heart of everything we do. Our goal is to ensure that every woman and family feels supported and heard, especially during challenging times.

At WAN Cymru, we strive to amplify the voices of women and address the diverse needs that affect our lives directly. WHAT WE DO

Advocacy and Support: We provide committed advocacy in key areas, such as health, education, and housing. Our services include assisting women with traumatic birth experiences, PTSD, postnatal depression, and difficulties in breastfeeding, as well as supporting those affected by stillbirths and miscarriages. We work tirelessly to ensure women have access to the resources and answers they need.

Empowerment Through Training: We believe in equipping women with the skills and confidence to advocate for themselves. Our training programs are designed to empower women, offering them the tools they need to become effective self-advocates and leaders within their communities.

Educational Events and Workshops: We offer a range of educational events, workshops, and training sessions aimed at fostering a culture of wellness. By raising awareness of key health risks and issues, we strive to promote healthy lifestyle behaviors and enhance health literacy among women.

OUR APPROACH

We are deeply committed to a community-centric approach, providing personalized support tailored to individual needs. Through comprehensive training and holistic education, we empower women to take control of their own lives and contribute to the well-being of their families and communities.

At WAN Cymru, we are dedicated to creating a supportive environment where women and families can thrive, overcome challenges, and make informed decisions about their lives. Join us in our mission to uplift and advocate for women, ensuring that every voice is heard and every need is met.
