About Wan Cymru

At WAN Cymru, we believe that every woman and family deserves a strong voice and unwavering support.

Founded by a passionate group of mothers and advocates, our organization is dedicated to empowering our community by addressing the issues that matter most to women and families. We understand that navigating life’s challenges can be daunting, which is why we provide a comprehensive range of services and resources. From health and wellbeing to education and employment, our mission is to ensure that every woman feels heard and supported during both good times and difficult moments.

Our Services

Our approach is grounded in collaboration and advocacy, combining face-to-face interactions with online support to meet the diverse needs of our community.

We strive to elevate the voices of women through training, mentorship, and self-advocacy, while also advocating for policy changes that promote equality and justice. At WAN Cymru, our core values—Empower, Elevate, Educate, Advocate, and Amplify—guide everything we do. We are committed to fostering a community where women and families can thrive, making informed choices that positively impact their lives. Join us as we work together to create lasting change and build a brighter future for all.
